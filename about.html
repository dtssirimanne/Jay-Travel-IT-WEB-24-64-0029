ABOUT

WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2002,we are corporate travel company providing travel management solutions to large domestic and global companies and touritis. We offer travellung services which includes ticketing, visa assistance , airport services and much more. JAY TROURS took flight, piloted by Hugo Silva, our CEO and Founder, with the main aim of becoming the best personalised travel agent in the world! This one-man show quickly gathered speed as the team at JAY TOURS grew, fast and wide.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the global leader in personalised, bestspoke tours. Together with our professional, friendly staff we are on the fastest track towards this.

Our Mission

Our mission at JAY TOURS is to create a memorable, personalised holiday experience for anyone who entrusts us with their vacation. As part of our growth, we also intend on adding more holiday destinations to our list; which will enable us to serve a larger client base.
