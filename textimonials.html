Rome

Rome, the Eternal City, is a captivating blend of ancient history and vibrant modern life. As you wander through its streets, you’ll encounter iconic landmarks like the Colosseum, where gladiators once battled, and the magnificent Pantheon with its towering dome. The Vatican City, home to the Pope, features the awe-inspiring St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel adorned with Michelangelo’s famous frescoes.
